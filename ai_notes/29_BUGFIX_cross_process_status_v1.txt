BUG: Live IME session always empty in Settings (beta 0.0.0.4)

================================================================================



SYMPTOM

  Instrumentation status showed zeros under "Live IME session" after typing.



ROOT CAUSE

  1. Android IME (LatinIME) runs in a different process than SettingsActivity.

     In-memory HeatmapWordSlotSession_v1 is always empty in the Settings process.

  2. DeviceProtectedUtils caches SharedPreferences per process; Settings could read

     stale snapshot/heartbeat even after IME wrote commits.



FIX (beta 0.0.0.5)

  - HeatmapCrossProcessPrefs_v1: fresh prefs read + commit() on IME writes.

  - HeatmapInstrumentationSnapshot_v2: status shows "Last commit from IME" from prefs.

  - Live section explains it cannot show live counters in Settings UI.

  - Auto-refresh status when opening Heatmap Smart Keyboard screen.



RETEST

  1. installDebugNoMinify (beta 0.0.0.5)

  2. Enable Heatmap KB (beta) — not store HeliBoard

  3. Type several words with space in Gemini/Notes

  4. Open Heatmap Smart Keyboard → Instrumentation status

  5. Expect "Last commit from IME" with words > 0 and IME active line
